# Cole's Mobile Detailing v6.2.2

- Submits a complete chatbot lead immediately after explicit contact authorization, even when the customer's final reply is only "yes."
- Uses deterministic extraction as a backup so ordinary or typo-heavy messages retain known service, vehicle, city, name, and contact details.
- Removes unverified "sent" claims from AI-generated text. Only a confirmed dashboard response can produce the green **Sent to Cole** card.
- Shows a visible **Not sent yet** warning and a retry button when dashboard delivery fails.
- Prevents an already-confirmed conversation from being submitted again.
- Replaces the hard-to-see service dropdown with clear tap-friendly service buttons.
- Restores required-field validation, highlights missing fields, and keeps customer details available after a failed send.
