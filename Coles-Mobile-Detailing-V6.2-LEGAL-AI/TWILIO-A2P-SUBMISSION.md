# Twilio A2P Submission — Owner Lead Alerts

Use this only for the current owner/authorized-staff internal alert program. Do not describe it as customer marketing or customer appointment texting.

## Campaign description

Cole’s Mobile Detailing uses this campaign to send transactional SMS notifications to the business owner and authorized staff members who have explicitly opted in. Messages notify staff when a customer submits a website form or chatbot inquiry and may include the customer’s name, requested service, vehicle information, and contact details so the business can follow up promptly. These messages are operational notifications only and are not sent to purchased lists or used for third-party marketing. Message frequency varies based on incoming leads. Recipients may reply STOP to opt out or HELP for assistance.

## Sample messages

1. `Cole’s Mobile Detailing: New website lead from [Customer Name]. Service requested: [Service]. Vehicle: [Year Make Model]. Phone: [Customer Phone]. Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help.`

2. `Cole’s Mobile Detailing: New AI handoff from [Customer Name]. Customer asked: “[Customer Question].” Phone: [Customer Phone]. Follow-up is needed. Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help.`

3. `Cole’s Mobile Detailing: Quote request from [Customer Name] for a [Vehicle] in [City]. Requested service: [Service]. Estimate status: [Estimate or Manual Review]. Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help.`

4. `Cole’s Mobile Detailing: A website lead needs manual review. Customer: [Customer Name]. Condition: [Condition Details]. Service: [Service]. Phone: [Customer Phone]. Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help.`

5. `Cole’s Mobile Detailing: New lead submitted through colesmobiledetail.com. Customer: [Customer Name]. Preferred date: [Preferred Date]. Service: [Service]. Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help.`

## Opt-in method

Select **Other** for the current internal alert campaign.

## Consent explanation

The recipient is the owner of Cole’s Mobile Detailing or an authorized staff member. The recipient explicitly opts in by personally providing and verifying their own mobile number, entering it into the private server configuration for the business lead-notification system, enabling operational SMS alerts, and confirming that they want to receive these alerts. No customer phone numbers are enrolled as recipients in this campaign. Messages are transactional internal notifications about new website forms and AI-assistant handoffs. Message frequency varies based on incoming leads. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for assistance. Privacy Policy: https://colesmobiledetail.com/privacy. Terms and Conditions: https://colesmobiledetail.com/terms. SMS program details: https://colesmobiledetail.com/sms-alerts.
